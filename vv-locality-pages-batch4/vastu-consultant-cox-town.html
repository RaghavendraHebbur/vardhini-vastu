Vastu in Cox Town: What Every Property Owner Should Know

Cox Town is one of Bangalore's most historic and prestigious inner-city residential localities — a colonial-era neighbourhood north of MG Road that has maintained its character as a premium, low-density residential address for Bangalore's established families, senior professionals, and the Anglo-Indian community that gave it much of its original character. Named after Mr. Cox, a Mysore State employee in the colonial era, the neighbourhood is known for its heritage bungalows, parish churches (St. Francis Xavier Church is a landmark), and the distinctive Bangalore bungalow architecture that defines pre-independence residential construction. Cox Town's central location, mature tree cover, and relatively low commercial intensity create one of Bangalore's most genuinely peaceful central residential environments.
