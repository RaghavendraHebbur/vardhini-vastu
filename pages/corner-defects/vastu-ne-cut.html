7 Proven Vastu Remedies for the North-East Cut

These zero-demolition remedies are used in the VIDS™ system to restore the vastu north east cut without any structural modification. Each remedy targets a specific layer of the defect.

Remedy 1: Virtual Gate Opening (VGO) at NE Boundary

Method: Place a pair of copper Vastu pyramids on either side of the NE boundary line — the internal edge of the cut. This creates an energetic "virtual gate" that restores the flow of Ishaan energy into the available zone. The pyramids are positioned at ground level or embedded in the wall/floor. This is the primary VIDS™ correction for plot-level NE cuts.

Remedy 2: Water Feature in the Available NE Zone

Method: Install a small water feature, aquarium, or decorative water bowl in the most NE-available corner of the home or plot. Water in the NE is one of the most powerful vastu activations — it amplifies the residual NE energy available even after a cut, partially compensating for the missing zone.

Remedy 3: Ishaan Yantra Placement

Method: Place a copper or brass Ishaan yantra at the NE point of the floor plan — either on the wall at the NE corner, or embedded in the floor facing North-East. The yantra energetically re-establishes the Ishaan deity's presence in the zone regardless of physical boundary limitations.

Remedy 4: NE Zone Colour Correction

Method: Paint the available NE walls in white, light blue, or pale green — Space and Water element colours that amplify what remains of the NE zone. Avoid yellow, orange, or red in the NE after a cut — these Fire element colours worsen the Space element deficiency.

Remedy 5: 9-Pyramid Copper Grid

Method: Install a 9-pyramid copper Vastu grid at the NE zone boundary. The grid distributes corrective energy across the missing zone footprint, creating a field of positive vibration that extends beyond the physical boundary. This is particularly effective for diagonal cuts where the zone is reduced rather than entirely absent.

Remedy 6: Fresh Plants and Natural Light Maximisation

Method: Place fresh, actively growing plants (holy basil/tulsi preferred, or any green leafy plant) in the available NE corner. Maximise natural light in the NE zone — keep the NE walls and windows clear of heavy curtains or obstructions. Living plants in the NE activate the growth and clarity energy of Ishaan even in a reduced zone.

Remedy 7: Clutter-Free NE Protocol

Method: When the NE zone is already reduced by a cut, any additional suppression through clutter, heavy furniture, or stored items in the available NE space compounds the defect dramatically. Keep the entire available NE zone absolutely clear — no storage, no heavy objects, no obstructions. This is the simplest and most immediately actionable step for any NE cut property.

Before Buying a Property: Vastu North East Cut Checklist

  • Check the plot shape on the registration documents — a diagonal boundary line in the NE indicates a cut
  • Inspect the floor plan for the NE corner — is it a clean right angle, or truncated?
  • Assess what occupies the NE zone — toilet, staircase, or store room here is a compounded defect
  • Calculate the percentage of NE zone missing — a small chamfer is less severe than a full missing quadrant
  • Check the NE boundary walls — are they higher than the SW walls? (Should be the reverse)
  • Verify underground water placement — is the sump or underground tank in the NE? (Positive vastu feature if so)
