South East Facing House Vastu: The Agni Direction – Complete Guide (2026)

The south-east direction in Vastu Shastra is governed by Agni — the fire deity — and Venus (Shukra), the planet of beauty, creativity, and passion. A south-east facing house channels powerful, active fire energy through its main entrance. This is not an inherently negative direction — but it is one that demands precise configuration to channel fire energy productively rather than destructively.

Incorrectly configured south-east facing homes produce symptoms like frequent quarrels, short tempers, financial instability, and health issues related to the fire element (acidity, inflammation, blood pressure). Correctly configured, they produce creative genius, passionate drive, financial success through effort, and vibrant household energy.

Room-by-Room Vastu for South East Facing House

Master Bedroom

South-west — unchanged. The Earth zone in the SW balances the intense fire energy of the SE entrance. This is critical for SE-facing homes — the master bedroom must be in SW to ground the residents emotionally and physically.

Kitchen

The kitchen should not be immediately adjacent to the SE entrance in a SE-facing home — this doubles fire energy at the front of the house. Instead, position the kitchen in the south-east of the interior floor plan, well away from the entrance foyer. The cook faces east.

Pooja Room

North-east interior corner. In a SE-facing house, placing a strong water-element pooja room in the NE is essential — it counterbalances the fire at the entrance and creates the ideal fire-water harmony throughout the home.

Water Feature

A water feature (aquarium, fountain, or bowl of water with flowers) in the north-east zone is strongly recommended for SE-facing homes. Water element in NE balances fire element at SE entrance — this is the single most effective passive remedy for SE-facing homes.

Living Room

North or north-west zones. Keep the living room cool in colour and energy — use blues, greens, and whites. This offsets the fiery SE entrance energy and creates a calm family environment.

Children's Room

West or north-west. Children in SE-facing homes can be more temperamental due to fire influence — the west zone provides the discipline of Saturn and cools the environment.

Bathroom / Toilet

North-west, west, or south (away from entrance). Never in north-east.

Staircase

South, south-west, or west zones. In a SE-facing home, stairs in the south-east must be avoided — this concentrates fire energy vertically through the home.

Key Remedies for South East Facing House
