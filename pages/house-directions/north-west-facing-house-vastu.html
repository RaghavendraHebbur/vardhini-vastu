North West Facing House Vastu: The Vayu Direction – Complete Guide (2026)

The north-west direction is governed by Vayu — the wind deity — and the Moon. In Vastu Shastra, Vayu energy is characterised by movement, change, communication, and social dynamism. A north-west facing house draws this mobile, connected energy through its main entrance, creating a home that is lively, socially active, and constantly evolving.

This makes NW-facing homes a double-edged orientation: the same Vayu energy that brings social success, business expansion, and creative inspiration can — if unchecked — create instability, restlessness, and a feeling that nothing settles. The VIDS™ corrections for NW-facing homes focus on anchoring Vayu energy with Earth and Water elements.

Who Benefits Most from North West Facing Houses

  • Import-export professionals — Cross-border movement aligns with Vayu's expansive reach
  • Politicians and social leaders — Moon governs public popularity and mass appeal
  • Travel professionals — Airlines, logistics, travel agencies, tourism
  • Communication professionals — Media, journalism, PR, marketing
  • Creative and intuitive professionals — Moon's influence supports intuition, creativity, and emotional intelligence

Main Door Placement in North West Facing House

The NW wall spans between north and west (between 292.5° and 337.5°). The main door in a NW-facing house should be positioned in the sub-padas closer to the north side of the NW wall — this captures more of Varun (north) and Kuber energy, moderating the pure Vayu energy and making it more stable and wealth-generating.

Room-by-Room Vastu for North West Facing House

Master Bedroom

South-west zone — as always. The Earth energy of the SW is the most important counterbalance to the Vayu energy of the NW entrance. Without a strong SW master bedroom, the household will feel perpetually restless and unsettled.

Kitchen

South-east zone. Fire element in SE is a stable, grounding presence that helps anchor the airy NW entrance energy. Cook facing east.

Pooja Room

North-east interior corner. The Water element and Jupiter energy of the NE pooja room work harmoniously with the Moon energy of the NW entrance — both are water-related planets, creating a spiritually harmonious home when both zones are active.

Living Room

North or east zones. The living room in a NW-facing home tends to be socially vibrant and popular — guests and visitors flow naturally through Vayu energy. Use this characteristic intentionally — a spacious, welcoming living room in the north or east amplifies the home's social success.

Guest Room

North-west zone (interior NW, away from entrance) or north. Vayu energy in NW keeps guests energetic and moving — they rarely overstay in NW-zone guest rooms. This is useful for hospitality businesses.

Children's Room

West or south-west secondary zones. Children need Earth-grounding energy for focus and stability — the further from the NW entrance, the better for study-focused children.

Home Office / Study

North zone — Kuber energy for wealth and professional success. The north office in a NW-facing home is a powerful combination: Kuber (north) and Moon (NW) together support business success, financial intuition, and public recognition.

Bathroom / Toilet

North-west (interior, away from entrance), west, or south zones. Keep north-east strictly free of toilets.
